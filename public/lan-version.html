Research & Development

Our current focus areas in space technology

Our R&D department is working on groundbreaking technologies that will enable sustainable space exploration and colonization. Using advanced simulation and AI-driven design, we're developing systems that can operate autonomously in the harshest environments.

From thermal regulation to radiation shielding, our integrated approach ensures all systems work seamlessly together to support human life beyond Earth.

Kronos Engine

Our next-generation fusion propulsion system designed for rapid transit throughout the solar system. The Kronos Engine features our OPTIMAL‚Ñ¢ heat distribution system with advanced thermal regulation for maximum efficiency.

Nebula 1

A closed-loop life support system capable of supporting a crew of 8 for up to 5 years without resupply. Nebula's biotechnology integrates algae and fungal systems for air revitalization and waste processing.

Aurora Launch System

Our heavy-lift reusable rocket designed for economical payload delivery to low Earth orbit. Aurora features our proprietary alloys and advanced aerospike engines for maximum efficiency across all flight regimes.

Hypercluster

A constellation of interconnected satellites providing high-bandwidth communications, precision navigation, and advanced Earth observation capabilities. Hypercluster will serve as infrastructure for future space development.
